background

    I like to go the movies on Tuesdays since it breaks up the week a little (and all tickets are half price). While anyone is welcome to join us (6pm in the Hoyts Chatswood Foyer), my good friends Matty and Emma are the only other people with the same level of dedication to this mid-week ritual.

    I thought I would create a page outlining my opinion of the movies I see. Since I prefer to know nothing about a movie before seeing it I can't say anything about the content of the movie. I think it's hard to compare all movies using one system so I don't give star ratings. I ended up with a page describing the lasting thoughts in my head after seeing each movie.

    "I am truly astonished by the sheer magnitude of your movie going experience. I'm not sure whether to applaud you or refer you to a relevant counsellor." said my friend David after reading this page.
